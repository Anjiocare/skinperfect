Growth Factor Concentrate

GFC is a non-surgical hair regrowth therapy where growth factors extracted from your own blood are injected into the scalp to stimulate inactive hair follicles.

MNRP + PRP

MNRP (Medical Nonsurgical Regenerative Procedures) combined with PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) is a treatment that uses your own blood to stimulate skin rejuvenation and hair growth.

Laser Hair Reduction

Laser hair reduction is a non-invasive treatment that uses laser energy to target and destroy hair follicles, resulting in reduced hair growth over time.

Skin Booster

Skin booster is a treatment that involves injecting hyaluronic acid into the skin to improve hydration, elasticity, and overall skin texture.

Hair Transplant

Hair transplant is a surgical procedure that involves moving hair follicles from one part of the body to another, typically to restore hair in areas where it has thinned or fallen out.

Hydra Facials

Hydra facials are a non-invasive treatment that combines cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, and hydration to improve skin texture and appearance.

Tattoo Removal

Tattoo removal is a process that uses laser technology to break down the ink particles in tattoos, allowing the body to naturally eliminate them over time.

Cautery

Cautery is a medical procedure that uses heat or electricity to destroy abnormal tissue or stop bleeding during surgery.

Botox

Botox is a cosmetic treatment that involves injecting botulinum toxin to temporarily reduce the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines.

Medi Facials

Medi facials are professional skincare treatments that use advanced techniques and products to deeply cleanse, exfoliate, and nourish the skin for a healthy glow.
